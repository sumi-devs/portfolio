ONGOING PROJECTS

FLOWER CLASSIFIER

A flower classification system designed to automatically identify different flower species from images. Leveraging convolutional neural networks (CNNs), it learns distinguishing patterns in shape, color, and petal arrangement. The model is trained on a curated dataset of labeled flower images and achieves high accuracy in recognizing multiple classes. It also incorporates computer vision to recognize flowers in te wild.

STORYGRAPH CLONE

This is a personal book-tracking and logging website developed as a full-stack application. Users can maintain a digital record of books they have read, are currently reading, or wish to read. It features user authentication, the ability to add reviews and ratings, and a clean dashboard for tracking progress.

STUDYHIVE

This full-stack web application creates a dedicated online environment to help students organize study sessions, track tasks, and collaborate with peers. The platform includes features such as group study scheduling, personalized to-do lists, resource sharing, and discussion boards.

IMAGE COLORIZATION

This project uses deep learning techniques to automatically colorize black-and-white images, giving them new life and realism. Based on encoder-decoder architectures and leveraging convolutional neural networks, the model learns to infer plausible colors for grayscale inputs.
